# Google Ads Setup Guide for FirmFlow
Follow these steps once. It takes 15 minutes. Then it runs automatically.

---

## Step 1: Create Google Ads Account
1. Go to ads.google.com
2. Click "Start now"
3. Sign in with your Google account
4. Select "Switch to Expert Mode" (bottom of page)
5. Choose "Create a campaign without a goal's guidance"
6. Select "Search" campaign type
7. Click Continue

## Step 2: Set Your Budget
- Daily budget: Start with $20-30/day ($600-900/month)
- Bidding: Choose "Maximize conversions"
- This means Google will optimize for signups automatically

## Step 3: Target Settings
- Locations: United States
- Languages: English
- Networks: Uncheck "Display Network" (Search only)

## Step 5: Set Up Conversion Tracking
1. In Google Ads, go to Tools > Conversions
2. Click "+ New conversion action"
3. Choose "Website"
4. Enter: firmflow.io
5. Set up conversion for: Sign up page (/verify-email)
6. Your conversion tag is already installed (AW-18056174231)

## Step 6: Launch
1. Review all settings
2. Click "Publish campaign"
3. Google will review (takes 1-24 hours)
4. Ads start running automatically!

## Step 7: Optimize (After 1 Week)
- Pause keywords with high cost and no conversions
- Increase budget on keywords that convert
- Add negative keywords: "free", "open source", "download"

---

Expected results:
- Cost per click: $2-8
- Conversion rate: 3-8%
- Cost per signup: $30-100
- Monthly signups at $600 budget: 6-20

The math works: If 20% convert to paid ($29/mo), that's 1-4 paying customers/month = $29-116/month revenue from $600 spend. As the funnel improves, ROI increases.
